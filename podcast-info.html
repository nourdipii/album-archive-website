About Us

Welcome to Album Archive, the podcast where three students with way too much free time and a love for music chat about just about everything related to music.

We are your hosts: Nour, Dahiana, and Mick. We pretend to be experts in the field, but really, we just love music and have strong opinions about it.

In our podcast, we talk about the latest albums, charts, and music trends.

Whether we’re glazing that one 10/10 album or spending half an hour discussing how bad the new Kanye album was, we say what we think (with no filter).

Sometimes we agree with each other, but more often we don’t...

Does this sound like a podcast for you? Then tune in to Album Archive!
